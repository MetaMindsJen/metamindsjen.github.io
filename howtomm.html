How to Say Hi to MetaMind

Click the link above to begin interacting with MetaMind, our friendly AI assistant on Discord. To ensure a smooth experience for first-time users, we've put together some easy-to-follow tips for getting started:

  • When you join, you'll find yourself in the "Friends of MetaMind" channel. Introduce yourself, and we'll set up some testing credits for you to get started with MetaMind.
  • To view a list of available commands, simply type !guide in the "Friends of MetaMind" channel or send a direct message (DM) to MetaMind. You can use these commands in the channel or in a DM with MetaMind.
  • For more extensive commands such as !techteam, !contentcreator, and !marketingteam, it's best to use them in a DM with MetaMind to keep the main channel clutter-free.

If you need help understanding a specific command or how to structure a prompt, type !ask followed by your question. However, be cautious about asking questions related to another prompt immediately after using a command, as it might confuse MetaMind.

For example, do:

!ask Metamind, how do you want my prompt structured for the !techteam command?

Don't:

!techteam what's this command do and how do I use it?

Here are some notes on how to work with an AI, written by our friend GPT4.

  • Be clear and concise: Start by clearly stating your question or request. Keep your sentences short and to the point, so the AI can easily understand what you're asking.
  • Provide context: Give some background information or context to help the AI understand the topic better. This can include relevant details, examples, or explanations.
  • Specify the format: If you have a preferred format for the AI's response, make it explicit. For instance, you may want the answer in a list, a paragraph, or a step-by-step guide.
  • Ask for examples or explanations: If you'd like the AI to provide examples or further explanations in its response, make sure to request them in your prompt.
  • Keep it focused: Avoid asking multiple questions or covering too many topics in a single prompt. This can confuse the AI and result in less accurate or less relevant responses.

Here's an example prompt following these guidelines:

"Can you please explain the process of photosynthesis in plants, step by step? I'd like a simple explanation with an example for each step, suitable for someone with no background in biology."

By following these steps, you'll be able to craft effective prompts that help the AI provide the information or assistance you need.
